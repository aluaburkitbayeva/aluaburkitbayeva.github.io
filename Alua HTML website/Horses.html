How Horses Help Release Stress

Riding or caring for horses involves physical activity, which releases endorphins, the bodyâ€™s natural stress relievers. Engaging in these activities helps lower cortisol levels (the stress hormone).

Horses are sensitive to human emotions and often mirror what a person is feeling. This connection can create a sense of being understood, which helps with emotional release and stress reduction.

Equine therapy, often used for people with PTSD, anxiety, or depression, involves therapeutic activities with horses. These programs have been found to reduce stress, increase emotional regulation, and promote healing.
